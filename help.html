Help Center

How to use NakshatraTalks · Common questions · How to get help

Welcome to the NakshatraTalks Help Center. This page covers how to get the most out of the app and what to do when something goes wrong. For account-specific issues or anything not covered here, email support@nakshatratalks.com.

Getting Started

How do I sign up?

Open the NakshatraTalks app, enter your phone number, and verify the OTP sent via SMS. Then complete a short profile (name, optional email, profile photo) and you're ready to browse astrologers.

What is an "intake profile"?

An intake profile is your birth information (date, time, place of birth) used by astrologers for accurate consultation. You can create profiles for yourself and family members, and select which one to use before each session.

How do I find an astrologer?

From the Home screen, browse top-rated astrologers, or use the Browse Chat / Browse Call tabs. Each astrologer card shows their specialization, rating, languages, and per-minute price. Tap a card to view their full profile.

Sessions

How do I start a chat or call?

From an astrologer's profile, tap "Chat" or "Call". Make sure your wallet has enough balance for at least one minute. The astrologer will receive your request and accept it within their availability window.

What happens if the astrologer is busy?

You'll see a queue confirmation modal showing your position and estimated wait time. You can choose to join the queue or pick another astrologer.

How is the session billed?

Sessions are billed by the minute, starting when both you and the astrologer are connected. The cost-per-minute is shown before you start. If your balance runs low, the session ends automatically. See our Terms of Service for full billing details.

How do I end a session?

In a chat, tap the menu and choose End Session. In a call, tap the red end-call button. You'll be asked to rate the astrologer afterwards.

Wallet

How do I add money to my wallet?

Open the Wallet tab, tap Recharge, choose an amount or enter a custom value, and complete payment via Razorpay (UPI, debit/credit card, or net banking). The balance reflects in your wallet within seconds.

I was charged but my wallet didn't update.

Razorpay reconciliation usually completes within 24 hours. If it doesn't, email support@nakshatratalks.com with your transaction ID (from your bank SMS) and registered phone number. See our Refund Policy for details.

Can I withdraw my wallet balance?

No. Wallet funds are non-transferable and can only be used within the App for astrology consultations.

Notifications & Permissions

I'm not receiving push notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings (Settings → Apps → NakshatraTalks → Notifications) and in the in-app notification toggle. If both are on and you still don't see them, try logging out and back in to refresh your device token.

The microphone permission was denied during a call.

Voice calls require microphone access. Open Settings → Apps → NakshatraTalks → Permissions and enable Microphone, then return to the app.

Account & Privacy

How do I update my profile?

Open Profile → Edit Profile. You can change your name, email, and profile photo. Phone number changes require contacting support.

How do I delete my account?

Use the in-app account-deletion flow described at delete-account.html. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.
