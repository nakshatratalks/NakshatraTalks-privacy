Cookie Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026 · Last Updated: May 8, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how NakshatraTalks uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (nakshatratalks.com) and what choices you have. The NakshatraTalks Android application itself does not use browser cookies, but it does store data locally on your device for similar purposes (described below).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your browser to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and help the site function. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and tokens stored by the mobile app.

2. Cookies on Our Website

Our marketing website uses a minimal set of cookies:

CookiePurposeDuration

We do not currently use cookies for advertising or third-party retargeting on our website.

3. In-App Local Storage (Android App)

The NakshatraTalks Android application stores the following on your device using secure storage and AsyncStorage:

  • Authentication tokens: JWT access and refresh tokens, stored in Expo SecureStore (encrypted at rest by the device OS). Required to keep you signed in across sessions.
  • FCM token: A unique push-notification identifier issued by Firebase, used to send you in-app notifications.
  • User preferences: Settings such as notification toggles, theme preference, and onboarding completion flags.
  • Cached data: Recently viewed astrologer information, intake-profile draft data, and similar non-sensitive data to make the app faster and work briefly when offline.

4. Your Choices

  • Website cookies: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies may break the site.
  • App local storage: Logging out clears authentication tokens. Uninstalling the app clears all locally-stored data. You can also use the in-app Account Deletion flow at delete-account.html to remove your data from our servers.
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications via your device's system settings or the in-app notification toggle.

5. Third-Party Services

The App and website may use third parties (Razorpay for payments, Firebase for notifications, Sentry for crash reporting, Supabase for data storage). Each operates under its own cookie/data practices. See our Privacy Policy for the full list and links.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
