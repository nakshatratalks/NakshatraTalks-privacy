Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about NakshatraTalks

About the Service

What is NakshatraTalks?

NakshatraTalks is an Android application that connects users with experienced astrologers for one-on-one consultations via chat or voice call. Sessions are billed by the minute from a prepaid wallet.

Are the astrologers real?

Yes. Astrologers on the platform are independent professionals who go through a basic onboarding and verification process. Each astrologer has a public profile showing their specialization, languages, experience, and aggregated user ratings.

Is astrology accurate or scientific?

Astrology is an interpretive practice and is not scientifically verifiable. Consultations on NakshatraTalks are intended for personal reflection and entertainment. They should not be relied upon as a substitute for professional medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice. See our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.

Account & Sign-In

Do I need to create a password?

No. NakshatraTalks uses phone-number-based sign-in via OTP (one-time password) sent over SMS. You don't need to remember a password.

I'm not receiving the OTP.

Check that your phone has cellular signal and that messages from unknown senders aren't being filtered to spam. If you still don't receive it after 60 seconds, tap "Resend OTP". If the issue persists, contact support@nakshatratalks.com.

Can I use the same phone number for both customer and astrologer accounts?

No. One phone number can be associated with only one role at a time. If you're a customer who wants to become an astrologer, please contact support — we'll arrange a controlled migration.

Wallet & Payments

What payment methods are supported?

All methods supported by Razorpay: UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.), debit and credit cards, net banking, and select wallet providers.

Is my payment information stored?

No. Card and UPI details are processed directly by Razorpay and are never stored on NakshatraTalks servers.

What's the minimum recharge?

The minimum recharge amount is shown on the Recharge screen in the app. We support common preset amounts and a custom amount above that minimum.

Can I get a refund?

Wallet recharges are generally non-refundable, with specific exceptions (failed recharge, platform fault, astrologer no-show). See our Refund Policy for the full conditions.

Sessions

How does the per-minute billing work?

Each astrologer publishes a price per minute (e.g., ₹15/min). Billing starts when both you and the astrologer are connected. Each minute of conversation deducts that amount from your wallet. If your balance drops below the cost of the next minute, the session ends automatically.

What if the astrologer doesn't accept my request?

If the astrologer doesn't respond within their availability window (typically 60 seconds), the request expires automatically and you are not charged.

The astrologer is online but my call disconnected mid-session.

Brief connection drops can happen on mobile networks. If you reconnect within a short window, the session resumes. If the session was clearly cut short due to a NakshatraTalks-side fault (not your network), email support@nakshatratalks.com with the session details and we'll review.

Can I record sessions?

The app does not provide a built-in session recording feature. Recording sessions without the astrologer's consent is not permitted under our Terms of Service.

Notifications

Do I have to allow notifications?

You can use the app without notifications, but you'll miss critical alerts (astrologer accepted your request, session ringing, low wallet balance). We strongly recommend enabling them.

Will I receive promotional messages?

By default, only service-related notifications are sent. Promotional notifications, if introduced in the future, will be opt-in or controllable via Settings.

Privacy

What data does NakshatraTalks collect?

Phone number, name, optional email, optional profile photo, intake profile (birth details), session history, payment transaction records, and basic device/usage information. The full list and explanation of how we use it is in our Privacy Policy.

Is my conversation with the astrologer private?

Conversations are stored securely on our infrastructure and are not shared with anyone except the astrologer you're consulting with. We do not advertise based on consultation content.

How do I delete my account?

Follow the in-app deletion flow described at delete-account.html.
