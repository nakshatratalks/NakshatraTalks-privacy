Delete Your NakshatraTalks Account

Last updated: April 19, 2026

At NakshatraTalks, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data from the NakshatraTalks mobile app (published by NakshatraTalks).

How to Request Account Deletion

To permanently delete your NakshatraTalks account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Open your email client and compose a new email from the email address (if any) or from any account you can access.
  2. Send the email to: nakshatratalks11@gmail.com
  3. Subject line: Delete My Account
  4. In the email body, include:
    • Your registered phone number (the one you use to log in)
    • Your full name as registered in the app
    • The reason for deletion (optional, helps us improve)
    • A confirmation statement: "I confirm that I want my NakshatraTalks account and associated data to be permanently deleted."
  5. Send the email. You will receive an acknowledgement within 2 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verified request, the following data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days:

  • Profile information: Full name, profile photo, date of birth, gender, email address
  • Birth details / Intake profiles: Date, time, and place of birth (used for astrology consultations)
  • Account credentials: Phone number and authentication records
  • Chat messages: All consultation chat history
  • Call logs: Duration and metadata of consultation calls (call audio is not recorded)
  • Session history: Records of past astrology sessions
  • Wallet balance: Remaining coin balance (non-refundable per our Terms)
  • Ratings and reviews: Reviews you have posted for astrologers
  • Push notification tokens: FCM device tokens used for notifications
  • App activity data: In-app actions and interaction history

What Data We Retain (and Why)

Certain data must be retained for legal, regulatory, and fraud-prevention reasons, even after account deletion. Retained data is anonymized where possible and not linked back to your identity.

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Transaction records (wallet recharges, payment receipts) 7 years Required under the Indian Income Tax Act and GST regulations
Anonymized session logs 1 year Fraud prevention, dispute resolution, platform safety audits
Payment gateway records (Razorpay) As per Razorpay policy Managed by Razorpay per RBI compliance requirements
Aggregated analytics (no personal identifiers) Indefinite Product analytics and service improvement — cannot be linked to you

Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgement: Within 2 business days of your request
  • Verification: We will verify your identity using your registered phone number
  • Deletion completion: Within 30 days of verified request
  • Confirmation: You will receive an email confirming deletion is complete
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted:
  • You will lose access to your chat history, session records, and wallet balance
  • Any remaining coin balance is forfeited and non-refundable
  • You cannot recover reviews, ratings, or past consultation records
  • You may create a new account in the future with the same phone number, but previous data will not be restored

Partial Data Deletion

Currently, we do not offer selective deletion of specific data types (e.g., deleting only chat history while keeping your account). If you wish to remove specific data, please contact us at nakshatratalks11@gmail.com and we will evaluate your request on a case-by-case basis.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 of India), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Board of India
