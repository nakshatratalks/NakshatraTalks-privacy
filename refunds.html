Refund Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026 · Last Updated: May 8, 2026

This Refund Policy explains when and how refunds are processed for transactions made within the NakshatraTalks mobile application. By recharging your wallet or using paid services in the App, you agree to this policy.

1. General Principle

NakshatraTalks operates on a prepaid wallet model. Users recharge their wallet through Razorpay, and the balance is consumed by per-minute astrology consultations. Wallet recharges are non-refundable except in the specific cases listed below.

2. Eligible Refund Cases

2.1 Failed or Duplicate Recharge

If your bank or UPI account is debited but your wallet balance is not credited within 24 hours, contact us. Such cases are usually resolved through Razorpay's automatic reconciliation, but if reconciliation fails, we will refund the affected amount to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

2.2 Verifiable Platform Fault

If a session is interrupted or fails entirely due to a verifiable NakshatraTalks server outage (not network issues on your device), we will, at our sole discretion, credit the affected portion of the session back to your wallet. We do not refund minutes that were genuinely consumed in conversation, even if you were dissatisfied with the quality of the consultation.

2.3 Astrologer No-Show

If an astrologer accepts your request but does not connect within the platform's connection-timeout window, no charge is applied. If billing was applied due to a system error in such a case, contact support for a full refund of those charges to your wallet.

2.4 Unauthorised Transactions

If you believe a wallet recharge was made by someone other than you (e.g., via a compromised account), notify us immediately at support@nakshatratalks.com. We will investigate in coordination with Razorpay and applicable law enforcement.

3. Cases That Are NOT Eligible for Refund

  • Dissatisfaction with the content or accuracy of an astrology consultation (consultations are subjective interpretations, not guaranteed predictions).
  • Sessions ended due to your network connectivity issues, device problems, or insufficient wallet balance.
  • Sessions you ended voluntarily before completion.
  • Wallet balance accumulated through promotional credits or referral rewards.
  • Recharges made more than 30 days prior to the refund request, except for verifiable failed-recharge cases.

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email support@nakshatratalks.com within 7 days of the disputed transaction.
  2. Include: your registered phone number, the transaction ID (visible in your wallet history), the date and time of the issue, and a brief description.
  3. Our support team will review and respond within 5 business days.
  4. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method (debit/credit card, UPI, net banking) and typically reflect within 7–10 business days, subject to your bank's processing time.

5. Withdrawals (Astrologers Only)

This section applies only to astrologers earning on the platform.

  • Astrologers may request payouts of accrued earnings via the in-app withdrawal flow.
  • Payouts are subject to KYC verification and a minimum withdrawal threshold disclosed in-app.
  • Once a payout is initiated, it is final. NakshatraTalks does not reverse approved payouts.
  • If an astrologer's account is terminated for fraud or violation of our Terms, accrued earnings may be forfeited subject to applicable law.

6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or card issuer without first contacting NakshatraTalks support, we reserve the right to suspend your account pending resolution. Frivolous chargebacks may result in permanent account termination and forfeiture of wallet balance.
